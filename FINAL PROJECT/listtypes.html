List Types

In HTML5, you can also add lists. You can add an unordered lists (<ul>), ordered lists (<ol>), and finally, description lists (<dl>).
As their name suggest, the unordered list organizes the lists with dots, and in the contrary, ordered list are listed in order (by default,
they are numbered with digits). And for the last type, they are ordered a bit differently. They are ordered with descriptions.
